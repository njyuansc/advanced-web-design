ABOUT US

Established in 1984, Ziger/Snead is a design-focused architecture firm based in Baltimore. Through dedication to a distilled design approach, focus on relationships, and active community engagement, the mission that great design can have a positive impact on our quality of life has resulted in transformative projects for many non-profits and institutions in Baltimore and beyond.
